Welcome to The Degenderer/Regenderer

Our platform allows you to create your own version of books where the characters are non-binary or have different genders than in the original. With our tool, you can explore the possibilities of gender in literature and create your own unique reading experience.

Imagine reading Romeo and Juliet with non-binary heroes, or The Handmaid's Tale with men as the victims. Or perhaps you'd like to turn Brokeback Mountain into a lesbian love story. With The Degenderer/Regenderer, the possibilities are endless.

If you're looking for books to degender/regender, you can find many of them on Project Gutenberg, a free online library with over 60,000 ebooks.

If you have your own book that you want to degender/regender, you can use our upload page to upload it and create your own customized version.
